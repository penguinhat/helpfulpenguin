What?

This is a web service that can turn a link into something easy to say

Why?

Sometimes you'll want to show someone a webpage but you don't want to deal with the kerfuffle of emailing someone a link. It's always better to talk in person but sharing internet links is something that's hard to do. Try saying http://www.amazon.co.uk/gp/product/B007HCCOD0/ref=s9_pop_gw_g349_ir04?pf_rd_m=A3P5ROKL5A1OLE&pf_rd_s=center-3&pf_rd_r=022GE1S720CBZ3PX3A18&pf_rd_t=101&pf_rd_p=358550247&pf_rd_i=468294 down the phone to your mum. It's not going to happen, is it?
